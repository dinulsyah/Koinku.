Koinku | Move Money Across Borders Quickly, Reliably, And For Fractions Of A Penny
Koinku is an open-source, decentralized protocol for digital currency to fiat currency transfers which allows cross-border transactions between any pair of currencies. Koinku protocol is supported by a nonprofit, the Koinku Development Foundation adn Koinku also known as platform that connects banks, payments systems, and people. Integrate to move money quickly, reliably, and at almost no cost.
